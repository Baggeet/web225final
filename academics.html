Accounting

You can strive for multiple levels of Accounting education here at RACC.

Accounting Transfer Program: Prepares students to transfer for a bachelor's degree.

Accounting Assosicate's Degree: Prepares you for employment in your career field.

Accounting College Certificate: Teaches the skills for entry-level employment

Web Site Development

You can strive for multiple levels of Web Site Development education here at RACC.

Web Site Development Associate's Degree: Prepares you for employment in your career field.

Web Site Development College Certificate: Teaches the skills to create and maintain a website

Currently there is no transfer program available for Web Site Development

Early Childhood Teaching

You can strive for multiple levels of Early Childhood Teaching education here at RACC.

Early Childhood Teaching Transfer Program: Prepares students to transfer for a bachelor's degree.

Early Childhood Teaching Associate's Degree: Prepares you for employment in your career field.

Early Childhood CDA Prep College Certificate: Teaches the skills for entry-level employment

Nursing

You can strive for multiple levels of Nursing education here at RACC.

Nursing (DAY) Associate's Degree: Prepares students to be employed as a nurse. Classes occur during the day

Nursing (EVENING) Associate's Degree: Prepares students to be employed as a nurse. Classes occur during the evening

Practical Nursing College Certificate: Prepares students to become a licensed nurse after 12 months of full time education

Administrative Office Specialist

You can strive for multiple levels of Administrative Office Specialist education here at RACC.

Administrative Office Specialist Associate's Degree: Provides students with necessary competencies to obtain employment as Administrative Specialists

Administrative Office Specialist College Certificate: Provides students with necessary competencies to obtain employment as Administrative Specialists

Currently there is no transfer program available for Administrative Office Specialist
